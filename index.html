Playnittude

Once upon a time, long before the creation of this pool, there was a future founder who was passionate about artificial intelligence. During the Covid crisis, he devoted his free time to studying the innovative algorithms recently used to lift the machine beyond human capacities for board games. One of these flagship algorithms is none other than the famous AlphaZero, the brainchild of engineers and scientists at DeepMind, which met outstanding success by allowing for the first time to beat the best Go player in the world.

He then felt the desire to create a small server whose particularity would be to cope with all kinds of board games and for which a similar AI would be in control. Later, it was only natural that this passion mingled with the founding lines of the ADAttitude pool. We are therefore happy to offer a challenge to all our delegators in order to increase their rewards.
