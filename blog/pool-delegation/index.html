Delegate to ADAttitude

Prerequisites

  • ADA stake
  • Yoroi wallet or Deadalus wallet

Delegate your ADA is safe and make the Cardano network more secure. You remain the owner of your ADA and they never leave your wallet. You can redelagate to another stake pool at any time provided a small cost fee (~0.17 Ada). When your stake is delegated, you still can sell or buy ADA with your wallet.

How to delegate with Yoroi wallet.

  1. Acces to your Yoroi wallet with your favorite browser.
  2. Choose the wallet you want to delegate and click on Delegation List
  3. Search with keyword adattitude, then click on Delegate
  4. Confirm with your password and click on Delegate
  5. Thank you! Your stake is now delegated to ADAttitude Stake pool.

How to delegate with Daedalus wallet.

  1. Open Daedalus wallet software and click on Delegation center
  2. Click on Stake pools and search with keyword ADAttitude
  3. Click on adattitude icon and click on Delegate to this pool
  4. Click on Continue
  5. Click on Continue
  6. Enter your password and click on Confirm
  7. Thank you! Your stake is now delegated to ADAttitude Stake pool.
